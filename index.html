WiDS Salt Lake City 2022 is an independent event that is organized by data scientists Sara Song and Jetta Hansen. WiDS Salt Lake City is part of the annual WiDS Worldwide conference organized by Stanford University at an estimated 200+ locations worldwide, which features outstanding women doing outstanding work in the field of data science. WiDS Salt Lake City was founded in 2019 by data scientist Jes Ford. All genders are invited to attend all WiDS Worldwide conference events.
